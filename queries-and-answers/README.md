## How to Use This Folder

1. **Create a new file:** `query-YYYY-MM-DD-topic.md`
2. **Write your question:** Be specific and clear
3. **Share with your AI:** I'll search your learning logs and provide answers
4. **Review the answer:** Save it here for future reference

## Tips for Good Queries

✅ **Be specific** - "What is the DWS system?" vs "Tell me about warehouse operations"
✅ **Ask what you forget** - That's the whole point!
✅ **Ask for clarification** - "Can you explain this concept more simply?"
✅ **Ask for connections** - "How does this relate to management?"
