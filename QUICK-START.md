# Quick Start Guide 🚀

## Your First Week (Today!)

### Step 1: Daily Learning Log (5 minutes)
**Do this EVERY DAY at the end of your shift**

1. Open `/daily-logs/` folder
2. Create a file: `2026-06-04-wednesday.md` (use today's date)
3. Write:
   - What you learned
   - Who taught you
   - Key takeaways
   - Questions you have

### Step 2: Weekly Review (15 minutes)
**Do this EVERY SUNDAY**

1. Open `/weekly-reviews/` folder
2. Create a file: `week-1-june-2026.md`
3. Review all 7 daily logs
4. Write:
   - Common themes
   - Skills you're developing
   - What confused you
   - Goals for next week

### Step 3: Monthly Progress (30 minutes)
**Do this on the LAST DAY OF EACH MONTH**

1. Open `/monthly-progress/` folder
2. Create a file: `june-2026.md`
3. Review all 4 weekly reviews
4. Write:
   - Major learnings
   - Progress made
   - Challenges faced
   - Next month's focus

## Ask Your AI Anytime

**When you forget something or need help:**

1. Go to `/queries-and-answers/`
2. Create a file: `query-2026-06-05.md`
3. Write your question
4. Share it with your AI (me!)
5. I'll search your learning logs and help you

**Example:**
```
Q: What were the 22 DWS slots for again?
A: [I'll search your logs and explain]
```

## Tips for Success

✅ **Be Honest** - Write what you really think, not just the "right" answer  
✅ **Be Specific** - Include names, numbers, and details  
✅ **Ask Questions** - Write down what confused you  
✅ **Be Consistent** - Do this EVERY DAY for best results  
✅ **Review Often** - Your past self is your best teacher
