## Welcome!

This is your personal learning hub where you'll track, organize, and review everything you learn as a management trainee. 

### How to Use This System:

#### 📅 **Daily (Every Day)**
- Write down 1-3 key things you learned
- Add your reflections and observations
- Store in `/daily-logs/` folder

#### 📊 **Weekly (Every Sunday)**
- Review your 7 daily logs
- Identify patterns and connections
- Update `/weekly-reviews/` folder

#### 📈 **Monthly (End of Month)**
- Analyze your 4 weekly reviews
- Track progress and skill development
- Update `/monthly-progress/` folder

#### 🔍 **Anytime - Ask Your AI**
- Search your learnings
- Get clarifications
- Review past concepts
